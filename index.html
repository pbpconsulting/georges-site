Our Story

Four Decades of
Timber Mastery

George Paphitis has spent over 40 years working with timber — first as a master heritage joiner and carpenter, crafting Victorian doors and sash windows across Adelaide. Now semi-retired, he channels that lifetime of skill into something a little closer to the kitchen table.

Every board that leaves George's workshop is made entirely by hand, from selecting the right timber to the final oil finish. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just the same pride in craft he's brought to every job for four decades.

When you buy a Knot Any Old Board, you're not buying something off a production line. You're buying a piece of genuine Australian craftsmanship — and probably the last breadboard you'll ever need.
